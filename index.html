How It Works

This tool saves you from memorizing long sequences of digits by turning it into a short, easy-to-remember phrase. No information is saved.

  1. Enter any sequence of digits
  2. Click Convert
  3. The corresponding phrase appears below
  4. Entering the phrase in the Input gives you the original digits.
    Separate words with space or period.

Note: Zeros at the beginning are ignored.
